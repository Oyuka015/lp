# Food Delivery App - Design System

## Design Philosophy

### Visual Language
- **Futuristic & Dynamic**: Inspired by sci-fi interfaces and electric acceleration
- **High-Tech Aesthetic**: Clean lines, minimal clutter, maximum impact
- **Energy & Speed**: Visual metaphors for rapid delivery service

### Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: Deep black (#0A0A0A) - creates premium, focused experience
- **Secondary Background**: Charcoal gray (#1A1A1A) - subtle depth and layering
- **Accent/Electric**: Neon cyan (#00D4FF) - primary action color, energy pulse
- **Secondary Accent**: Electric pink (#FF006B) - highlights and secondary actions
- **Text Primary**: Pure white (#FFFFFF) - maximum contrast and readability
- **Text Secondary**: Light gray (#B0B0B0) - supporting information

### Typography
- **Display Font**: "Orbitron" - futuristic, geometric, high-tech feel for headings
- **Body Font**: "Inter" - clean, highly readable sans-serif for content
- **Accent Font**: "JetBrains Mono" - monospace for numbers, prices, technical data

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Used Libraries & Effects
1. **Anime.js**: Smooth micro-interactions and state transitions
2. **Pixi.js**: Particle systems for energy effects and background animations
3. **ECharts.js**: Data visualization with neon color schemes
4. **Splide.js**: Smooth image carousels for food galleries
5. **Matter.js**: Physics-based animations for cart interactions
6. **P5.js**: Dynamic background patterns and audio visualization
7. **Shader-park**: Electric pulse effects and gradient flows

### Animation & Interaction Patterns
- **Electric Pulse**: Buttons and interactive elements pulse with neon glow
- **Particle Streams**: Background particle systems suggesting speed and energy
- **Smooth Transitions**: Page transitions feel like sliding between dimensions
- **Hover Effects**: 3D tilt and glow effects on food cards
- **Loading States**: Electric arc animations during data loading

### Header & Navigation Effects
- **Sticky Navigation**: Translucent black background with neon accent line
- **Active States**: Electric cyan underline for current page
- **Cart Counter**: Pulsing neon circle showing item count
- **Profile Avatar**: Glowing border when logged in

### Component Styling
- **Cards**: Dark background with subtle neon border glow on hover
- **Buttons**: Gradient from dark to electric cyan with glow effect
- **Forms**: Dark inputs with cyan focus states and floating labels
- **Search Bar**: Translucent with electric border on focus
- **Category Pills**: Rounded with electric accent when active

### Background & Atmosphere
- **Primary Background**: Solid deep black throughout application
- **Decorative Elements**: Subtle geometric patterns in dark gray
- **Particle System**: Slow-moving cyan particles suggesting movement
- **Gradient Accents**: Subtle electric gradients on section dividers

### Data Visualization Style
- **Chart Colors**: Neon cyan and pink with transparency
- **Grid Lines**: Dark gray for subtle structure
- **Hover States**: Bright cyan highlights
- **Typography**: Monospace font for numerical data

### Mobile Considerations
- **Touch Targets**: Minimum 44px with generous padding
- **Gesture Support**: Swipe navigation for food categories
- **Performance**: Optimized particle counts for mobile devices
- **Typography**: Scalable font sizes for various screen sizes
